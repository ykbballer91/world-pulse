# AI/RAG Reference-Data Freshness Private Briefing Draft

This is an internal two-page draft. It is not public material, not a finished external document, and not a sales deck.

## Page 1: What World Pulse Reviews

### One-Line Summary

World Pulse compares selected public reality records with activity in structured reference systems to review reference-data freshness.

### The Problem

AI/RAG systems rely on reference systems, but reference systems do not update evenly across domains or event types.

For some domains, a source-recorded event may be followed by readable movement on related reference pages. For other domains, the page set may be noisy, unavailable, or too location-dependent to explain cleanly.

World Pulse reviews those differences as retrospective reference-system activity.

### World Pulse Approach

- Select source-recorded reality events.
- Link them to reviewed candidate reference pages.
- Measure pageview windows around event dates.
- Score internal examples for clarity and communication safety.

### What This Is

- Internal review method.
- Source-linked reference-system activity check.
- Candidate freshness-window analysis.
- Retrospective review of selected event-page pairs.

### What This Is Not

- Not real-time attention measurement.
- Not causality.
- Not prediction.
- Not emergency or action relevance.
- Not market relevance.
- Not model-quality diagnosis.

### Why AI/RAG Teams Might Care

AI/RAG systems often depend on structured reference sources. If reference systems show uneven activity after source-recorded events, teams may want a careful way to review which event categories and reference pages are easier to evaluate.

World Pulse does not diagnose a model. It reviews whether event-linked reference pages show measurable activity in a selected daily window.

## Page 2: Current Best Internal Evidence

### Current Best Category: Historical Space Weather

Historical Space Weather is currently the clearest internal category because:

- Candidate reference pages are cleaner than earthquake candidates.
- Candidate pages are less location-dependent.
- Several source-recorded samples score highly under internal criteria.
- Pageview-window movement is easier to explain in selected samples.

This does not mean Space Weather is the final product category. It is the current best test bed for AI/RAG reference-data freshness exploration.
